Our Story

A taste passed down with care

Rosalie's Kakanin began the way most good kakanin does — in a home kitchen, with rice soaked overnight, banana leaves washed and warmed over a flame, and a recipe that's been adjusted by feel for years before it was ever written down.

Today, every tray of bibingka, every block of suman pinipig, and every batch of puto pao leaving our kitchen at Super NLEX Patubig, Marilao is still made the same way: slowly, by hand, using real coconut milk, fresh muscovado sugar, and ingredients we'd be happy to serve our own family.

A product of RDC Corporation, we are committed to using only the highest quality ingredients — freshly made everyday, perfect for any occasion, from our home to yours.

— Rosalie & the Kakanin team
